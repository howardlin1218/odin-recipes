Popcorn Chicken

Decription

Recipe on how to make the famously delicious Taiwanese Popcorn Chicken!

Ingredients

  • Cut pieces of chicken
  • Flour
  • Garlic
  • Buttermilk
  • Vegetable oil
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Dip chicken in buttermilk
  2. Cover chicken in flour
  3. Heat up the oil
  4. Deep fry the chicken in the oil
  5. Add garnish made of garlic and any other Ingredients
  6. Prepare sauce for dipping
  7. TIME TO SERVE!!!
