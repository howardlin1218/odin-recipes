Beef Noodles

Decription

Recipe on how to make the famously delicious Taiwanese Beef Noodle Soup!

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Beef
  • Vegetables
  • Soup Base
  • Thick Noodles
  • Eggs (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil the Water
  2. Add the soup base
  3. Cook the noodles
  4. Cook the beef
  5. Put the cooked noodles into the boiled soup
  6. Put the cooked beef into the noodle soup
  7. (optional) cook the egg in the noodle soup
  8. TIME TO SERVE!!!
